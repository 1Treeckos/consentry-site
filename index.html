Consentry

Cookie consent for Shopify stores that actually blocks tracking until visitors choose — built on Google Consent Mode v2 and Shopify's own privacy system, so your analytics keep working.

How it works

Consentry installs as a theme app embed: one toggle in the theme editor, no code. It shows a consent banner only in regions where your store requires it, keeps analytics and marketing tags blocked until the visitor decides, records the choice through Shopify's customer privacy system, and lets visitors change their mind anytime. Uninstalling leaves nothing behind.

See it working — proof, not promises

Set a private code in the app's settings, then open any page of your store with ?consentry_debug=YOURCODE — a live status panel shows exactly what is blocked and allowed, in real time. Only people with your code can see it.
